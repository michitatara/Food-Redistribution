What is Shafoo?

Our Mission is simple: We know tens of thousands of food gets wasted. So we decided to find that food new purpose. Now, that food can feed the homeless and the needy.
Americans waste 150,000 TONS of food daily.
The United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) estimates that 1 in 8 households have struggled with food insecurity recently.
