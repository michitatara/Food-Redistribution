General Information for Prospective Donors

"Donors are part of the fabric of our mission." We wouldn't be able to do this without you. Thank you to each and every one of you for making a contribution.

Our Two Paths for Food Donors

Transit Path

  1. Our more widespread option
  2. All you need is this app on your phone and your excess food.
  3. Our app will connect you to the nearest less fortunate fellow.
  4. Please try to deliver this food to a nearby fellow within 24 hours.

Hub-Centric Path

  1. Submit our forms regarding some basic information.
  2. We might ask for pictures,barcodes,etc. of food to ensure food quality
  3. One of our team members will come in <36 hours to pick up your excess food to deliver it to our select hub spots.
